Workflow System

The Post-Production Pipeline

A systematic 5-phase editing pipeline built to guarantee professional quality and zero revisions.

1

Ingestion & Backup Cataloging Day 1

Accepting camera logs and raw files. Cataloging metadata, sound syncs, backing up projects locally and creating proxy templates for speedier timelines.

2

Rough Cut Sequence Assembly Day 2-3

Curating the core narrative story. Dropping dead spaces, selecting custom royalty-free music and pacing the cuts with beat synchronization to maintain viewer retention.

3

Sound Design Synthesis (SFX) Day 4

Integrating multi-layered sound design. Adding swooshes, ambient layers, risers, clicky overlay sound effects, and normalizing dialogues with noise reductions.

4

DaVinci Resolve Color Grading Day 5

Converting log profiles using node graphs. Balancing exposures, matching cameras, skin tone tracking, and grading visual colors to express story vibes.

5

Motion VFX & Final Delivery Day 6

Embedding final motion graphics, lower thirds, title cards, digital zooms, and render exports in ultra high definition (4K Prores/H.264).
